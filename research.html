I completed my undergraduate studies at the University of Warwick, with a final-year research project on point symmetries of crystallographic defects in diamond.

I moved to Imperial College London for my postgraduate study. In my master's degree I undertook a research project on optimising x-ray flux from high-harmonic generation. I am now a PhD student in the astrophysics group, working on understanding substructure in protoplanetary discs - specifically those with large scale asymmetries, and using hydrodynamics and/or radiative transfer simulations to explain them.
